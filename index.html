  • How we think

Deep Change Model

We employ a blend of design thinking and systems thinking to achieve results at massive scale.

Transdisciplinary

We have thinkers from radically different backgrounds working on the same problems together, to yield the most innovative solutions.

Built with scale in mind

We're thinking about solutions we can research, build, test and scale for every country. We think deeply, then deploy and scale globally.

Systems, not just solutions

We're trying to put in place systems for change, not just one time solutions. We want out generation and every future generations to benefit from progress made on the SDGs.

Radical collaboration

Building long term solutions for the SDGs requires collaboration among governments, the private sector and everyday people. We focus on collaborating with other groups, think tanks and government officials to provide the best results.
